FAQ

FAQ

Quick answers to the most common questions before you request a quote.

Do I need technical knowledge? +
No. I handle the technical side. You only approve content and design.
What if I already have a site? +
We’ll review it. If improving makes sense, we optimize. If replacing is better, we do it—no drama.
How fast can you deliver? +
Depending on the plan: 5, 10 or 14 business days (if content arrives on time).
Does it include domain and hosting? +
Yes. We include a .com domain for 1 year and hosting. Renewals stay under your ownership.
Can the site be in English? +
Yes. We do ES/EN with a toggle for tourist‑friendly or international businesses.
What does “Google Ads ready” mean? +
We set up tracking + structure for campaigns: Tag Manager, events and a dedicated landing page (by plan).
How does payment work? +
50% upfront, 50% on delivery. Payment methods: bank transfer, PayPal, and Sinpe (CR).
What do you need from me to start? +
Photos, services, coverage area, hours and your WhatsApp. If you have a GBP, the link helps.
How long does it take to launch a website? +
Most sites go live in 5–14 business days, depending on the plan and how fast content is provided.
Do I need to provide photos? +
Photos help a lot, but if you don’t have them we can start with professional stock images and replace them later.
Can I update the site later? +
Yes. You can request updates anytime. We can also set up a simple maintenance plan if you want ongoing help.
Do you manage Google Ads? +
Ads management isn’t included, but the Growth plan is structured for future campaigns (tracking, events and landing page).
